Tourist places of India

Jagannath Temple

Built in the 11th-century, Jagannath Temple is the most sacred pilgrimage
site in East India. Given the honour of being of one of the Char Dhams,
the shrine is one of the most-visited Hindu temples in India. Read more

Khanduala Waterfall

Some 32 km from the enticing setting of Karlapat Wildlife Sanctuary
there is located the famous Khanduala Waterfall. This charming waterfall
is one of the sought-after tourist places in the region. Read more

Konark Temple

A UNESCO World Heritage Site and a popular temple in Odisha is Konark Sun
Temple. This enticing tourist attraction is a monumental representation
of Lord Sun's chariot with massive wheels and horses. Read more

Dhauli Giri

Some 8 km south of Bhubaneswar, there is located Dhauli Hills. A must-visit
tourist attraction located on the banks of river Daya. Dhauli is known for
its famous attraction, Pagoda Peace, also known as Shanti Stupa. Read more

Hirakud Dam

Hirakud Dam,the biggest dam in the world, is built across the Mahanadi
River in the Sambalpur district of the Indian state of Odisha. It drains
an area of 1,33,090 Sq.Kms., more than twice the area of Srilanka . Read more

Chillika Lake

Island boat tours with potential dolphin and migratory bird sightings amidst serene lake settings; known for seafood delicacies and peaceful beach experiences.Read more

Nandankanan Zoological Park

Gopalpur Beach
